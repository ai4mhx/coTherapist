# coTherapist

A copilot for Mental Healthcare Experts - An AI-powered assistant designed to support mental health professionals in their practice.

## Overview

coTherapist is an innovative tool that assists mental healthcare experts by providing intelligent support, insights, and assistance during therapy sessions and clinical work. This copilot aims to enhance the quality of care while reducing the administrative burden on healthcare professionals.

## Features

- **Intelligent Support**: AI-powered assistance for mental health professionals
- **Session Enhancement**: Tools to augment therapy sessions effectively
- **Clinical Documentation**: Streamlined documentation and note-taking capabilities
- **Evidence-Based Insights**: Access to research-backed treatment approaches
- **Privacy-Focused**: Built with patient confidentiality and HIPAA compliance in mind

## Requirements

- Mental healthcare professional credentials and training
- Understanding of ethical AI usage in healthcare settings
- Compliance with local healthcare regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, etc.)

## Privacy & Ethics

coTherapist is designed with the following principles:

- **Patient Privacy**: All patient data is handled with strict confidentiality
- **Professional Judgment**: The tool is an assistant, not a replacement for professional expertise
- **Ethical AI**: Transparent, explainable AI that supports clinical decision-making
- **Compliance**: Adheres to healthcare data protection regulations
